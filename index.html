Architecture

This is the only logically correct clock. By eliminating arbitrary 60-base divisions, it synchronizes human perception with the decimal arithmetic of modern computation.

CONVERSION_LOGIC
Tdec = [(h×3.6M + m×60K + s×1K + ms) / 86.4M] × 100M
  • h, m, s, ms: Standard parts (24h)
  • 86.4M: Total ms in standard day
  • 100M: Total units in decimal day
